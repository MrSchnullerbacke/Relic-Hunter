GAME MECHANICS

Movement Top-down directional movement. Player can dodge using a dash
ability.

Combat Player attacks enemies using abilities granted by relics. Enemies
attack through melee, ranged, or area attacks.

Damage Types Physical Fire Frost Storm Poison Void

Status Effects

Burn Damage over time.

Freeze Short immobilization or heavy slow.

Shock Chain lightning effect to nearby enemies.

Poison Stacking damage over time.

Void Drain Life-steal or health absorption effect.

Critical Hits Chance for attacks to deal increased damage.

Cooldown System Certain relic abilities trigger cooldown timers before
reuse.

Area Effects Some attacks create zones such as fire pools or gravity
fields.

Summoning Relics can spawn allied units that attack enemies
automatically.

Risk Mechanics Certain relics grant powerful bonuses with drawbacks such
as reduced health or self-damage.

RELIC SYSTEM OVERVIEW

Relics define nearly all player power.

Categories:
1. Weapon Relics – primary attacks
2. Element Relics – add elemental effects
3. Modifier Relics – change behavior of abilities
4. Summon Relics – spawn allies
5. Passive Relics – improve survivability or utility
6. Risk Relics – powerful effects with drawbacks
7. Legendary Relics – run‑defining effects

Relics also use internal tags:
projectile, melee, summon, aura, fire, frost, storm, poison, void, crit, dash, aoe.

These tags allow automatic synergies between relics.

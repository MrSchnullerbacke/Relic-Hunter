GAME ENTITIES

Player The controllable character. Has movement, health, relic slots and
combat abilities.

Enemy Hostile unit with defined behavior patterns and attack types.

Elite Enemy Stronger enemy variant with additional modifiers and better
loot drops.

Boss Large enemy encounter that ends a dungeon layer or biome.

Projectile Temporary entity created by attacks such as arrows, spells,
or energy bolts.

Summon Friendly helper unit spawned by relics.

Relic Core gameplay item that grants abilities, modifiers, or passive
effects.

Room A single dungeon space containing enemies, events, puzzles, or
loot.

Dungeon Collection of connected rooms forming a level.

Shrine Interactive object granting temporary buffs.

Shop Merchant location where relics or healing can be purchased.

Event Object Special interactive objects such as cursed chests or relic
altars.

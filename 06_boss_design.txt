BOSS DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Bosses are designed to test builds rather than simply having high HP.

Key rules:
- Clear attack telegraphs
- Multiple phases
- Unique arena mechanics
- Specific gameplay challenge

Example Bosses:

Bone King
Skeleton ruler who summons minions.

Spider Matriarch
Fast boss creating web traps and spider swarms.

Colossus Sentinel
Huge stone guardian with heavy attacks.

Inferno Titan
Controls lava zones and fire bursts.

Relic Warden
Endgame boss that adapts to the player's relic build.

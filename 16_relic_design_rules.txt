RELIC DESIGN RULES

Relics are the core gameplay system of Relic Hunter.

Design Principles

1.  Every Relic Must Change Gameplay Relics should modify how the player
    fights rather than only adding numerical bonuses.

Examples: Good: - projectiles split - attacks explode - enemies are
pulled toward the player

Weak: - +5% damage - +3% speed

2.  Clear Role Each relic belongs to one category: Weapon Element
    Modifier Summon Passive Risk Legendary

3.  Use Tag System Relics use tags to allow system-based interactions.

Example tags: projectile melee summon aoe fire frost storm poison void

Tags allow synergy without manually defining every combination.

4.  Risk vs Reward Some relics should offer strong effects with
    drawbacks.

Example: High damage but lower health Powerful ability with longer
cooldown Area damage that slightly hurts the player

5.  Build Identity Relics should naturally lead players into
    recognizable build types such as: summoner builds projectile builds
    element builds control builds

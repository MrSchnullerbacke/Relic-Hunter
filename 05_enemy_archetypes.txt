ENEMY TYPES

Grunt
Basic melee enemy.

Ranged
Attacks from distance.

Tank
Slow but durable.

Swarm
Many small enemies.

Summoner
Creates additional enemies.

Caster
Uses magical attacks.

Support
Heals or buffs other enemies.

Pressure Enemy
Forces player movement with area attacks.

Denial Enemy
Blocks areas or projectiles.

Elite
Rare stronger variant with special modifiers.

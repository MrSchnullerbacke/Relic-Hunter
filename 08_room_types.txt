ROOM TYPES

Combat Room
Standard fight.

Treasure Room
Contains relic reward.

Elite Room
Harder fight with better loot.

Shop Room
Merchant selling relics or healing.

Shrine Room
Temporary buffs.

Puzzle Room
Environmental puzzle challenge.

Event Room
Random special encounter.

Trap Room
Environmental hazards.

Mini‑Boss Room
Mid‑dungeon boss.

Boss Room
Final fight of biome.
